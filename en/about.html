About

Angelo Bruno Rizzo is a photographer by passion, deeply connected to the sea, the memory of places, the Italian Navy, diving, and travel.

His photography is born from silent observation: details, horizons, faces, harbors, ships, seascapes, and fragments of daily life become images capable of telling time and identity.

Graduated in Economics and Commerce, he has worked in the banking sector since 1997. Alongside his profession, he has always cultivated an interest in photography, the sea, naval culture, Greece, and the underwater world.

An Advanced Diver and holder of a sailing and motor boating license beyond twelve miles, he combines the gaze of a traveler, the discipline of a man of the sea, and the sensitivity of one who observes before taking a photograph.

“Photography does not stop time: it listens to it.”Angelo Bruno Rizzo
